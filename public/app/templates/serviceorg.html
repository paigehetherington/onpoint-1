• Project Buena Vista

    Medical outreach to provide free and effective health care to people who have minimal access. Communities located in the rainforest must have medical care in order to protect their environment. Project Buena Vista beleives in a conservation mission which provides educational opportunities and health care to the people who live in the rain forest.

    Region: Southeastern Peru

  • Acupuncture Relief Project

    Since 2008, the Acupuncture Relief Project has provided over 140,000 treatments to patients living in rural villages outside of Kathmandu Nepal. Our efforts include the treatment of patients living with HIV and AIDs as well as people suffering from extreme poverty and social disfranchisement.

    Region: Nepal

  • Acupuncturists Without Borders

    AWB provides relief and recovery in the wake of major disasters and traumatic events. Clinics utilize auriuclar acupuncture to treat post traumatic stress. AWB provides trainings for their volunteers and also offers clinics for veterans. Their work is domestic and international.

    Region: US, Haiti, Nepal, Mexico, Mongolia, Greece

  • Mindful Medicine Worldwide

    Description: MMW brings long-term integrative health care to people of developing areas, by establishing and operating free integrative health care clinics. Thier current focus is on supporting areas affected by the earthquakes of 2015.

    Region: Nepal, Thailand

  • Global Acupuncture Project

    The PanAfrican Acupuncture Project trains local healthcare workers to use simple and effective acupuncture techniques that enable them to treat the symptoms associated with HIV/AIDS, malaria, TB, and other delibilitating conditions.

    Region: Africa, Mexico

  • Projects Abroad

    Professional acupuncture volunteers work at a private hospital in Kathmandu, Nepal.

    Region: Nepal

  • Barefoot Acupuncturists

    Barefoot Acupuncturists’ objective is to offer an efficient and affordable medicine to society's poorest and least fortunate. In order to treat populations who have insufficient access to healthcare, they open, operate and oversee low cost acupuncture clinics in underprivileged areas. They alsotrain local people in acupuncture who will be able to practice in their own communities.

    Region: India

  • Acupuncture Ambassadors

    Researching the spread and growth of acupuncture’s role in humanitarian efforts.

    Region: Nepal, Spain, Japan, Jordan

  • Global Clinic

    Global Clinic is committed to advancing a global network that creates practical and sustainable health solutions for those in need. The pursuit of Global Clinic is to provide and maintain a sustainable network of integrative, holistic medical practices to populations with limited or no access to conventional healthcare. Acupuncture is one of the modalities they provide in their clinics.

    Region: Dominican Republic, Haiti, Cuba, Ecuador, Romania, Guatemala, India

  • Guatemala Acupuncture and Medical Aid Project

    GUAMAP is a Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) acupuncture training project that uses a “barefoot doctor” model for rural community health care.

    Region: Guatemala

  • One World Health Project

    OWHP’s mission is to positively impact global health by promoting wellness education and increasing access to acupuncture in under served communities.

    Region: Peru, Sri Lanka, Kenya, US

  • World Medicine


    World Medicine is a registered UK charity providing acupuncture to people around the world suffering the effects of trauma, disaster and poverty.

    Region: India
